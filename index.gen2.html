Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend routine check-ups and cleanings every 6 months for optimal oral health. Patients with specific concerns may need more frequent visits.

Are your services covered by insurance?

Many dental insurance plans cover our preventive and restorative services. Please contact us with your insurance details for verification.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Last-minute cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes! We provide emergency care for dental pain, trauma, and urgent issues. Please call us immediately at +91 99999 99999.

Are your facilities modern and hygienic?

Absolutely! We use advanced technology and maintain strict sterilization protocols to ensure patient safety and comfort.

What are payment options?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and can discuss flexible payment plans for complex treatments.
